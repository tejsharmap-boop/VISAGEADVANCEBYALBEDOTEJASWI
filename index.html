WATER TEMPERATURE THERAPY
When to use hot vs cold water on your face — timing matters
🔥 HOT WATER (38–42°C)
Use ONLY for deep cleansing — opens pores to remove blackheads, sebum, makeup residue
Pre-shaving: warm water softens beard hair → less irritation, cleaner shave
Before clay/charcoal mask: open pores first for deeper extraction
NEVER use daily — destroys skin barrier, strips natural oils, causes dryness
Max 2× per week, max 30 seconds on face, always follow with cold
❄️ COLD WATER (10–15°C)
Morning routine: cold splash wakes you up, reduces puffiness, tightens pores
After hot cleanse: always finish with cold to close pores — lock in clean
After workout: cold water reduces inflammation, prevents breakouts from sweat
Ice cube method (3–5 min): rubbing ice on face boosts circulation, firms skin over time
Under eyes daily: reduces dark circles and morning puffiness immediately
Best habit: end every shower with 30s cold water on face — daily use is safe
